FRC is an international high school robotics competition. Each year, teams of high school students, coaches, and mentors work during a six-week period to build robots capable of competing in that year's game that weigh up to 125 pounds

First Lego Leauge or FLL is an important program that allows 4-8th graders to build design and compete with their own robots. WAMS has an FLL team.
